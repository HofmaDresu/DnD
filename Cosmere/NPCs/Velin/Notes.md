# Velin
## Characteristics
Wize, no-nonsense, generous

## Goal
Get the caravan survivors to safety and report what happened

## Appearance
Velin (VEH-lin; he/him) is an Alethi human with weather-beaten ski, graying hair, and piercing green eyes. A grizzled veteran of many trips across the Shattered Plains, he wears the knee-length coat he was issued when he was a soldier in the Kholin army

## Description
Velin is a lighteyes of the eighth dahn, retired from lilitary service. He is a committed defender of the chulls, wagons, and travelers he serves. Dispite his gruff exterior, he is well-loved by those in the caravan
